ReSpace AI
Mobile prototype · reshuffle & redesign · iOS + Android

Design notes

A high-fidelity, clickable prototype of ReSpace AI — the same React app running live in both an iOS and an Android frame.

System

  • Type: Space Grotesk for display, Inter for body — from the ElkaTech system.
  • Accent: a calm teal, with a warm amber reserved for the future Redesign (shopping) path so the two modes never feel the same.
  • Shape: soft 16–26px cards, gentle shadows, lots of breathing room — the friendly, trustworthy feel you asked for.
  • Trust layer: every layout pairs an illustrated render with a precise top-down floor plan; AI output is always reviewable and labelled as a suggestion / estimate.

Try this

  • Tap New project (the centre button) and walk the full reshuffle flow.
  • On the Results screen switch between Grid / Swipe / Compare.
  • Open a layout → Modify to see the AI-edit sheet.
  • Use Jump to screen above to drive both phones at once.
  • On Home, preview the empty state; toggle Light/Dark any time.
  • From Home, open Redesign for the measurement + shopping path.
